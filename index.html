December is DeDeDoMo!

Deliberately-Decide-to-Document Month, or #DeDeDoMo, is a time to spend extra effort improving your organization’s written documentation. It recurs each December.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is DeDeDoMo pronounced?
Say “dee-dee-doh-moh,” exactly how it looks like it would be pronounced in English.
Does DeDeDoMo have anything to do with NaNoWriMo?
Not really! NaNoWriMo is National Novel Writing Month, the time of year (November) when aspiring authors make a concentrated effort to write a novel. This popular phenomenon inspired DeDeDoMo in concept, name, and decentralized nature. There is no other affiliation.
Who is behind this?
DeDeDoMo is decentralized and owned by its participants. This website is maintained by these people with these contributors.
